About me

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Agricultural and Resource Economics at the University of California, Berkeley.

I am interested in data-driven projects that will help policy makers take better informed decisions. My current research focuses on the economic challenges associated with energy needs and energy provision. In particular, my dissertation is (I) assessing the economic impact of climate change on electricity markets, and (II) the role of market integration in this context.
