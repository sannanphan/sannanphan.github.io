I am a PhD candidate in Agricultural and Resource Economics at the University of California, Berkeley.

My research is mostly focus on the economic challenges associated with energy needs and energy provision. In particular, my current project is (I) assessing the economic impact of climate change on electricity markets, and (II) the role of market integration in this context.
